01

A living understanding of your career

After the discovery call, Profound stays current through lightweight periodic check-ins - tracking not just who you are, but what you're navigating right now. A leadership challenge, a promotion ceiling, a new direction.

A real agent would know. Your AI rep should too.

02

Connections based on what you're navigating

Most platforms match people by job titles. Profound can also match by situation. If a senior PM is struggling to move into leadership, the system introduces them to someone who navigated that exact challenge six months ago.

The warmest introductions, and strongest relationships, come from shared experiences.

03

Capturing breakthroughs

When someone moves past a plateau, the agent asks a simple question: what helped you break through? With permission, those insights are anonymized and stored.

Over time, Profound builds a growing library of real breakthroughs - what worked, what didn't, and how people actually move forward.

04

A confidential layer for communities

Even in trusted communities, people hesitate to openly discuss career struggles. Profound acts as a confidential intermediary—users share with their AI rep, and the system introduces relevant peers, or anonymized insights.

Connections happen quietly, with context.
